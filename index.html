I was homeless.

My name is Vontae Diggs, and I am a future NFL Football player. I graduated the University of Connecticut with a degree in Human Development and Family Studies. My degree is one of purpose. Soul searching. Intrinsic. After college, I became an undrafted Free Agent. I have been invited to several NFL training camps, multiple times, being the last person released. Being that close to your dream would have stopped most people. But I am not most people.

Over the last five seasons I have played in the USFL with the New Orleans Breakers and in the CFL with the Edmonton Elks and the Toronto Argonauts. My workout regimen has become somewhat of an urban legend. Many professional football players have asked to participate with me in the off-season. This solidifies my belief of me being on the correct path to accomplish my goals, as current players look to me as a resource. My stats are easy to find. My coaches will always tell you that on film, "Diggs is always around the ball."

Often in an interview the Coach or GM asks about adversity. I have learned firsthand what it takes to overcome adversity. I was homeless growing up. I lived on a park bench. I grew up in a broken environment. I saw my peers die. I have overcome that struggle of finding my place in the world through football. Football is my vehicle. I am passionate about helping other kids and families as you can see by my charitable work in the BLOG section and my thoughts from my Twitter feed in my NETWORK. My message is on by bicep for the world to see.

Every day I go to work, I am able to keep the practice, game or training session in perspective. What you will not see are flashy tackles. What you will not see are miraculous interceptions. In the VIDEO section you will see a consistent linebacker whose tackles are calm, quiet, and sound. This demonstrates my ability to read the play and the correct path I take in responding. I know my playbook. I know my opponents’ playbook. I know where to be and when to be there.

When I put on your jersey you know you will be getting a consistent, grounded, and hardworking linebacker who takes nothing for granted. I look forward to sharing in success with your club. After all, we are all looking for family. Some just want that more than others.
